Why We Require Your Company Information & Tax ID

As a B2B frame moulding supplier, we work exclusively with registered businesses. To ensure a smooth and compliant purchasing process, we require your company details and tax ID for the following reasons:

  1. Business Verification We only sell to verified businesses, not individual consumers. Your company information helps us confirm that you are a legitimate business partner.
  2. Sales Tax Compliance If your business qualifies for tax-exempt purchases, a valid Tax ID is required for proper documentation and compliance with state and federal tax laws.
  3. Accurate Invoicing & Legal Documentation Providing your business details ensures that invoices and order confirmations are correctly issued for accounting and legal purposes.
  4. Trade Credit & Payment Terms For businesses that qualify for net payment terms (e.g., Net 30, Net 60), verifying company details helps us assess creditworthiness and reduce the risk of unauthorized purchases.
  5. Protecting Wholesale Pricing To maintain fair pricing for our B2B customers, we prevent unauthorized resellers and individuals from taking advantage of wholesale discounts meant for businesses.

By requiring this information, we can streamline your order process, ensure tax compliance, and maintain secure business transactions.
